# Analytics Suggestions for Sales Dashboard

Based on your email, calls, and combined data, here are analytics features you can implement:

## üìä Email Analytics Enhancements

### 1. **Time-Based Performance Analysis**
- **Best Time to Send**: Analyze which days/times have highest open rates
- **Response Time Analysis**: Time between send and first open
- **Engagement Decay**: How engagement drops over time after send
- **Peak Performance Windows**: Identify optimal sending windows

### 2. **Content Performance**
- **Subject Line Analysis**: Track performance by subject line patterns
- **Email Template Performance**: Compare different email templates
- **Content Length Impact**: Analyze engagement vs email length
- **CTA Performance**: Track click rates by call-to-action type

### 3. **Recipient Segmentation**
- **Industry Performance**: Engagement rates by company industry
- **Company Size Analysis**: Performance by company size/revenue
- **Geographic Performance**: Engagement by location/region
- **Title/Role Analysis**: Which job titles engage most

### 4. **SDR Performance Deep Dive**
- **SDR Efficiency Score**: Sends per hour, engagement per send
- **SDR Improvement Trends**: Week-over-week performance changes
- **Best Practices Identification**: What top SDRs do differently
- **SDR Workload Analysis**: Balance of sends across team

### 5. **Predictive Analytics**
- **Engagement Prediction**: ML model to predict open/click probability
- **Optimal Send Time**: AI recommendation for best send times
- **Churn Risk**: Identify prospects likely to disengage
- **Conversion Probability**: Predict likelihood of positive response

## üìû Calls Analytics Enhancements

### 1. **Call Performance Metrics**
- **Call Volume Trends**: Daily/weekly/monthly call patterns
- **Connect Rate Analysis**: Best times/days for connections
- **Call Duration Insights**: Optimal call length for outcomes
- **Follow-up Patterns**: Time between calls and outcomes

### 2. **Disposition Analysis**
- **Outcome Distribution**: Breakdown by disposition type
- **Conversion Funnel**: Visualize journey from call to outcome
- **Disposition Trends**: How outcomes change over time
- **SDR Disposition Patterns**: Individual SDR outcome preferences

### 3. **Call Quality Metrics**
- **First Call Success Rate**: Outcomes on initial contact
- **Follow-up Effectiveness**: Success rate of follow-up calls
- **Call Frequency Impact**: Optimal number of calls per prospect
- **Time-to-Conversion**: Days from first call to positive outcome

## üîó Combined Analytics Enhancements

### 1. **Multi-Channel Attribution**
- **Email + Call Impact**: Combined effect on engagement
- **Channel Preference**: Which prospects prefer email vs calls
- **Sequence Effectiveness**: Best email-call sequences
- **Touchpoint Analysis**: Optimal number of touches before conversion

### 2. **Account-Level Insights**
- **Account Engagement Score**: Combined email + call engagement
- **Account Health Dashboard**: Overall account status
- **Multi-Contact Accounts**: Performance of accounts with multiple contacts
- **Account Journey Mapping**: Visualize touchpoint timeline

### 3. **ROI & Efficiency Metrics**
- **Time Investment Analysis**: Time spent vs outcomes
- **Cost per Engagement**: Calculate cost efficiency
- **Pipeline Velocity**: Speed from first touch to opportunity
- **Revenue Attribution**: Link activities to revenue
